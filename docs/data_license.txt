Data usage and licensing notes for `oir_flatmount_segmentation`.

1. Source data:
   - OIR retinal flatmount images and masks generated by the Hartnett Lab and collaborators.

2. Data access:
   - Data are not redistributed in this bundle.
   - Access requires approval by the data owners and any relevant institutional governance.

3. Permitted use:
   - Non-commercial research and educational use unless otherwise approved in writing.
   - Users must comply with institutional and sponsor requirements.

4. Restrictions:
   - No attempt to re-identify protected study information.
   - No redistribution of derived annotations without explicit permission.

5. Attribution:
   - Cite the associated publication and acknowledge the Hartnett Lab.
